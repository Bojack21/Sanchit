My Core Values

I believe in freedom - both financial and personal - giving me the ability to explore new ideas, take risks, and control my own journey. I thrive on curiosity, always seeking to push boundaries and discover new paths. Leaving my consulting job to fully dive into the world of crypto was one of the biggest leaps of faith I've taken, and it taught me the power of believing in myself.

Community and connection are essential to my happiness. Whether it's through shared experiences or just being present, I value the joy that comes from meaningful relationships and love to make people laugh.

Living with integrity is my non-negotiable. I strive to have a positive impact without causing harm to others, leading a life that is not only fulfilling for myself but also uplifting for those around me.

Truths I Hold Sacred

Success should never come at the cost of others. Achieving my goals is important, but it's equally vital to uplift the people around me.

Life isn't just about chasing more - it's about knowing when you've reached "enough." I want to be a present family member, caring for my loved ones and truly being part of their lives. Family, friends, and relationships are my anchors.

Finding balance between ambition and contentment is key to happiness. Progress and peace need to coexist.

What I'm Truly in Pursuit Of

I'm always chasing personal growth - whether it's exploring new ideas, mastering new skills, or testing the limits of my abilities. I want to continue pushing boundaries while also contributing to the progress of others.

Freedom drives me - the freedom to be curious, to connect with others authentically, and to constantly learn. I'm especially excited about how AI is reshaping the world and its potential to change the way we live, work, and interact.

I'm passionate about everything from crypto to memory sports, from gaming to financial independence. Learning never stops for me, and I love diving deep into different worlds, whether that's AI or mastering a new game.

I'm in pursuit of balance - balancing ambition with joy, innovation with stability, and personal achievement with nurturing strong, loving relationships.
